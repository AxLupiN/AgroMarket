About the site!

We're a team of developers - Sujen Man Chitrakar, Rojen Dangol, and Sandesh Shahi, led by our proficient project manager, Shaswot Ghimire, and supported by our insightful business analyst, Shubash Singh. Our mission is simple yet impactful: to bridge the gap between local farmers and consumers, fostering a direct and transparent exchange. By connecting you directly with the source of your food, we aim to promote sustainability, support local economies, and provide you with fresh, quality produce straight from the farm to your table. Join us in cultivating a healthier, more vibrant community, one harvest at a time.
